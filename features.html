CloudShow Features

Flexible & Easy to Setup

Use CloudShow to power a menu board at your restaurant, a promo screen in reception, an analytics dashboard for production, or anything else you can think of.

Download the client for your device, pair your device with CloudShow, and you're up-and-running within minutes!

Over 60 Apps and Counting

CloudShow supports all the major social platforms, like Twitter, Instagram, Yelp, and more.

You can also upload PowerPoint slideshows, videos, PDFs, audio files, images, and much more to show on your digital signs.

With over 60 apps available today and more being added every month you have the power to display anything you need.

Scheduling

Use the default simple schedule to show one layout all the time, or use detailed schedules to show different layouts at different times of the day, or different days of the week

Create as many layouts as you need and schedule everything exactly how you want it.

Templates

Use templates to simplify your device management. Create a template and use it on multiple devices with one-click.

Update your template and all assigned devices will receive the new content in realtime for easy management.

Widgets

Extend your sign's layout with widgets.

Widgets can overlay extra information on your sign, like the time, date, weather, and more. You can even add background music and audio to almost any app.

Manage from Anywhere

Manage your devices from anywhere in the world.

Need to update a sign while you're travelling? No problem, it's just a few clicks away. View your device map to get a quick overview of the status and location of all your devices.

Realtime Sign Updates

Update your device's configuration and your digital sign is updated automatically. There's nothing to update or install each time, it just works like magic.

Change a layout or schedule, add an app, re-arrange the images in your slideshow and watch as your digital sign is updated in realtime right next to you or on the other side of the world.
